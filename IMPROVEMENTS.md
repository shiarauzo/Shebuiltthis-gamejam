# IMPROVEMENTS.md — Ranked Code Backlog for the Deadline

**Game:** Notebook Awakening (Godot 4.5.1, top-down survival, HTML5/itch.io)
**Jam:** She Built This — theme *Awakening*. Voted on **Theme, Creativity, Visuals & Audio, Gameplay, Fun** + special judge prize **"Most Interesting Art Style"** (interesting ≠ pretty).
**Scope of this doc:** code-only changes I (the implementer) can ship today — GDScript, procedural `_draw()`, shaders, tweens, tuning, accessibility, UX. **Excludes** anything needing AI-generated or hand-drawn image/audio assets (those are the user's separate tasks). All art proposals below are **procedural / code-drawn** and therefore jam-legal.

## How this is ranked
Ranked by **impact-per-effort for winning votes**, not by raw impact. The art-style prize and "first-30-seconds" hook are weighted up per the brief, because (a) the procedural doodle is this entry's single biggest differentiator, and (b) jam voters rate dozens of games for ~2 min each, so the first impression disproportionately drives the score. Note also itch.io's **Bayesian rating adjustment penalizes games below the median vote count** — so "frictionless to play and finish" (mobile, accessibility, clarity) indirectly protects every category score by widening who completes and rates the game. ([itch rating system](https://itch.io/jam/coco-code-gamejam-1/topic/4569793/how-does-the-rating-system-of-itchio-work))

Effort key: **S** ≈ <30 min · **M** ≈ 30–90 min · **L** ≈ 2–4 h.

## Notes on constraints honored
- **No AI / no hand-drawn assets:** every visual item above is procedural `_draw()`, shader, or tween. The boil, paper-grain, walk, wobble, and telegraph are all generated in code.
- **GL Compatibility renderer (web):** CanvasItem fragment shaders (#1 procedural-shader variant, #8) are supported; the noise-displacement boil can alternatively be done purely in `_draw()` vertex jitter (#1 as written) to stay 100% safe on low-end web GPUs — recommended.
- **Audio/art files** remain the user's tasks (#9 only wires the hooks; #1–#8 deliberately avoid needing any asset).
