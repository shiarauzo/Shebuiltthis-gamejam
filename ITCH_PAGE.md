## Description (page body)

You're a stick figure, scribbled in the margin of a half-finished notebook page. The student
wandered off — and you just **woke up**.

But you're not alone on the page. A giant pencil comes hunting, scribbling lines of ink that
**hurt to touch**. Dodge its strokes as the page fills up around you. Then the notebook's other
half stirs: a giant **eraser** awakens, chasing everything and rubbing the world away. Survive it,
race to the edge of the page, and **leap out** before the notebook slams shut behind you.

A tiny survival-dodge game about a drawing that refuses to be forgotten.

**Made in 48 hours for She Built This: Game Jam (powered by Sentry). Theme: Awakening.**

### How to play
- **Move:** WASD / Arrow keys — or **drag** on a touch screen
- **Dodge** the pencil's ink (watch for the flashing telegraph before each stroke)
- You have **3 lives** — each hit fades your figure; when you fade out, you're gone
- Survive the eraser, then reach the **glowing right edge** to escape
- Your score is **how long you survived**

### Tips
- A moving doodle is a safe doodle — the pencil is slower than you, so keep moving.
- When the eraser wakes up, don't panic: it sweeps the ink away too, opening safe lanes.
