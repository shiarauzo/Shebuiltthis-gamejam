Art / Visuals
  - HAND-DRAWN by Shiara Arauzo during the jam:
      * the player doodle — a 7-frame run animation (source in doodle-run/,
        background-keyed + ink-tinted by tools/process_doodle.gd into
        assets/sprites/doodle_run/).
      * the eraser (assets/eraser.png).
  - Drawn procedurally in-engine (GDScript _draw): ink strokes, the page
    border, and the decorative page doodles (star, spiral, cloud, squiggle).
  - THIRD-PARTY assets (pre-existing, NOT created by us — credited per jam rules):
      * Graph-paper background (assets/sprites/grid_paper.jpg) —
        "Cuaderno papel textura limpio cuadrado hoja en blanco" via Vecteezy,
        https://es.vecteezy.com/arte-vectorial/7385469-cuaderno-papel-textura-limpio-cuadrado-hoja-en-blanco
        (used under the Vecteezy Free License, attribution required).
      * Pencil (assets/pencil.png) — reference image found via Pinterest,
        https://i.pinimg.com/736x/bb/1b/b0/bb1bb08588a30c285cae8fe4a3a07444.jpg
        (original creator unknown; credited to its source as found).
  - The sketchy heart health icons (assets/sprites/heart_full.svg,
    heart_empty.svg) were generated procedurally with rough.js, a
    deterministic geometry library — NOT generative AI. The generator
    script is included at tools/heart-gen/ and can be re-run to
    reproduce the SVGs exactly.
  - NO generative-AI art was used (per jam rules).
