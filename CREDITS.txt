MADE DURING THE JAM
------------------------------------------------------------
Game design, code, and all visuals
  - Shiara Arauzo

Art / Visuals
  - The player doodle is HAND-DRAWN by Shiara Arauzo: a 7-frame run
    animation drawn during the jam (source frames in doodle-run/). They
    are mechanically recolored to the game's ink tone and background-keyed
    by tools/process_doodle.gd into assets/sprites/doodle_run/ (no AI).
  - The rest of the graphics are drawn procedurally in-engine (GDScript
    _draw): the pencil, eraser, ink strokes, graph-paper sheet + grid,
    and the decorative page doodles. Created for this jam.
  - The sketchy heart health icons (assets/sprites/heart_full.svg,
    heart_empty.svg) were generated procedurally with rough.js, a
    deterministic geometry library — NOT generative AI. The generator
    script is included at tools/heart-gen/ and can be re-run to
    reproduce the SVGs exactly.
  - NO generative-AI art was used (per jam rules).
